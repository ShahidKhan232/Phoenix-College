Complaint Form

Use this form to report any issues, grievances, or complaints related to college facilities, staff, academics, ragging, harassment, or any other matter. Your complaint will be handled confidentially by the Grievance Redressal Committee / Principal's office.

Important Information:

  • All complaints are taken seriously and investigated promptly.
  • You can file anonymously if you prefer (but providing contact details helps faster resolution).
  • For ragging, sexual harassment, or serious matters – use this form or contact the Anti-Ragging / Internal Complaints Committee directly (see Important Links or Contact page).
  • Response time: Usually within 7–15 working days (depending on nature of complaint).
  • Track your complaint: Note down any reference number you receive after submission.
