Important Links

Quick access to official websites, portals, and resources useful for students, parents, and staff of My College.

College & Official Links

  • My College Official Website: www.mycollege.edu.in (this site)
  • College Notice Board (Physical): Check near main entrance / admin block
  • College Library OPAC (if available): library.mycollege.edu.in (internal catalog – ask librarian)
  • College Alumni Portal: alumni.mycollege.edu.in (register for updates & events)

University & Board Links

  • University of Mumbai (Degree Courses): https://mu.ac.in/
  • Mumbai University Digital Portal (Results, Timetables, Hall Tickets): mum.digitaluniversity.ac
  • Maharashtra State Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education (HSC – Junior College): https://mahahsscboard.in/
  • MSBTE (Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education – Diploma): https://msbte.org.in/

Admission & Scholarship Portals

  • FYJC Centralized Admission (Junior College): https://11thadmission.org.in/
  • DTE Maharashtra (Diploma Centralized Admission Process – CAP): https://poly24.dtemaharashtra.gov.in/ (update year as needed)
  • MahaDBT (Maharashtra Government Scholarships): https://mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in/
  • National Scholarship Portal (Central Govt Schemes): https://scholarships.gov.in/

Other Useful Resources

  • National Digital Library of India (NDLI): https://ndl.iitkgp.ac.in/
  • UGC Website (Higher Education Guidelines): https://www.ugc.gov.in/
  • AISHE (All India Survey on Higher Education): https://aishe.gov.in/
  • Anti-Ragging Helpline (UGC): https://www.antiragging.in/ or call 1800-180-5522
  • Women Helpline (Maharashtra): 181 or womenhelpline.in

These links are regularly checked and updated. If any link is not working, please inform the college office. Bookmark this page for quick access.
