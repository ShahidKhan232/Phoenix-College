Scholarships & Financial Aid

We support our students through various government and college scholarships. Apply early to get benefits for tuition fees, books, and other expenses.

Available Scholarship Schemes

1. Post Matric Scholarship for SC/ST/OBC/SEBC/EBC (MahaDBT)

For students from reserved categories (SC, ST, OBC, SEBC, EBC) pursuing Junior College, Diploma, or Degree courses. Covers tuition fees, exam fees, maintenance allowance, etc.

  • Eligibility: Maharashtra domicile, family income limits apply (e.g., OBC < ₹8 lakh), minimum marks required.
  • Benefits: Full/partial tuition waiver + allowance up to ₹10,000–₹20,000 per year (varies by category/course).

Apply through official portal: MahaDBT Portal

2. State Government Open Merit Scholarship

For meritorious students (open category) who passed HSC (Class 12) with good marks and pursuing higher education (Degree/Diploma).

  • Eligibility: Maharashtra resident, minimum 60% in HSC, pursuing recognized courses.
  • Benefits: ₹100–₹500 per month (or fixed amount for tuition/books).

Apply via: MahaDBT Portal

3. Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship for College and University Students (PM-USP)

Central Government scheme for meritorious students from low-income families pursuing Degree courses.

  • Eligibility: Top performers in Class 12 (80th percentile or above), family income < ₹4.5 lakh/year.
  • Benefits: ₹12,000 per year for first 3 years, ₹20,000 in 4th year (for general degree); higher for professional courses.

Apply through: National Scholarship Portal (NSP)

4. Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship (for SC Students)

For SC category students excelling in studies, pursuing higher education (Degree/Diploma).

  • Eligibility: Maharashtra SC students with high marks in previous exams.
  • Benefits: Financial aid for books, supplies, and tuition (up to ₹5,000–₹10,000 per year).

Apply via: MahaDBT Portal

5. Assistance to Meritorious Students (Junior Level)

For top-ranked students in Class 11-12 (Junior College) from Maharashtra.

  • Eligibility: High achievers in SSC/HSC exams.
  • Benefits: Up to ₹2,300 per year or more for meritorious students.

Apply through: MahaDBT Portal

6. Minority Scholarships (Post Matric)

For minority community students (Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain, Parsi) pursuing higher studies.

  • Eligibility: Maharashtra domicile, minority category, income limits.
  • Benefits: Tuition + maintenance allowance.

Apply via: MahaDBT Portal

College-Level & Other Support

Our college offers freeships for girls in aided sections (Junior College), merit-based aid, and help with application for above schemes. Contact office or visit scholarship counter during admission.

Main Application Portals:

  • MahaDBT (Maharashtra Govt Scholarships): https://mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in/
  • National Scholarship Portal (Central Schemes): https://scholarships.gov.in/

Last dates usually March–October. Check portals for 2026-27 updates. Documents needed: Income certificate, caste certificate, marksheets, Aadhaar, bank details.

For help with applications or eligibility, visit the college office or contact: scholarship@mycollege.edu.in
