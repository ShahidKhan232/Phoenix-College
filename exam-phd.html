PhD Examinations & Evaluation (2026)

PhD program evaluation includes coursework exams, progress reviews, pre-submission presentation, thesis evaluation, and viva-voce as per University of Mumbai guidelines.

Key Stages & Timeline (Typical for 2026 Candidates)

  • Coursework Exams: Conducted after 6–12 months of registration. Subjects: Research Methodology + Subject-specific paper. Usually held in June / December sessions.
  • Progress Review / RAC Meetings: Every 6 months. Submit progress report and presentation before Research Advisory Committee.
  • Pre-PhD / Synopsis Presentation: After completing coursework and 2+ years of research. Submit synopsis and defend publicly (usually 2026–2028 for current batch).
  • Thesis Submission: After approval of synopsis. Submit 4–5 hard copies + soft copy to University.
  • Thesis Evaluation & Viva-Voce: External examiners review thesis. Viva-voce (open defense) scheduled within 3–6 months of submission (typically 2028–2030 for 2026 entrants).

Important Notes

  • All exams and evaluations follow UGC 2022 Regulations and University of Mumbai PhD Guidelines 2025–26.
  • Coursework passing: Minimum 55% aggregate required.
  • Plagiarism check mandatory (using UGC-approved software) before thesis submission.
  • Check Mumbai University portal for exact dates, forms, and notifications: mu.ac.in or mum.digitaluniversity.ac

For your personal timeline, supervisor guidance, or RAC meeting dates, contact the Research Cell or your PhD guide. Good luck with your doctoral journey!
