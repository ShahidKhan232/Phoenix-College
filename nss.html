National Service Scheme (NSS)

Our NSS unit promotes community service and social awareness. Strength: 200+ volunteers.

Activities Provided by the College

  • Annual 7-day Special Residential Camp in adopted villages (e.g., cleanliness drives, watershed management, tree plantation).
  • Regular activities: Blood donation camps, health awareness programs, beach/river clean-up drives.
  • Awareness campaigns: Road safety, AIDS/HIV, drug abuse, Swachh Bharat, environment conservation.
  • Campus work: Garden development, playground maintenance, poster/essay competitions.
  • Community service in adopted areas: Ration/medicine distribution, literacy programs, yoga days.
  • Participation in state/national events (e.g., Republic Day Parade selections, Youth Festivals).

Fees & Enrollment

Completely **free** for all enrolled students (boys & girls). No fees for camps or activities. Uniform/materials provided by college/NSS unit.

Join NSS to earn certificates and develop leadership skills. Contact NSS Program Officer for enrollment.
