College Prospectus 2026-2027

Welcome to My College – Your gateway to quality education in Mumbai. This prospectus provides all essential information about our institution, courses, admission process, fees, and facilities.

1. About the College

My College is a well-established educational institution in Mumbai, Maharashtra, offering Junior College, Diploma, and Degree programs. We are affiliated to the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education (for Junior College), MSBTE (for Diploma), and University of Mumbai (for Degree courses). Our focus is on holistic development, academic excellence, and preparing students for future challenges.

  • Established: 1985
  • Location: Near Main Road, Andheri East, Mumbai - 400069
  • Accreditation: NAAC B Grade (example – update with actual)
  • Campus: 15 acres with modern infrastructure, labs, library, sports ground, and canteen

2. Vision and Mission

Vision: To be a leading institution empowering students through quality education and values for a better society.

Mission:

  • Provide affordable and inclusive higher education
  • Foster academic, skill-based, and character development
  • Promote research, innovation, and community service
  • Encourage discipline, ethics, and lifelong learning

3. Courses Offered

Junior College (FYJC & SYJC – HSC)

Streams: Arts, Commerce, Science (with optional IT/Bifocal subjects)

Diploma Courses (3 Years – MSBTE Approved)

Branches: Computer Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication, Mechanical, Civil, Information Technology

Degree Courses (3 Years – University of Mumbai)

B.A. (Arts), B.Com. (Commerce), B.Sc. (Science with subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Computer Science)

4. Admission Process

  • Junior College: Through centralized online admission process of Maharashtra Govt. (FYJC portal). Merit-based on SSC marks.
  • Diploma: Through centralized admission by DTE Maharashtra (CAP rounds). Based on SSC marks / entrance if applicable.
  • Degree: Online registration via Mumbai University portal. Merit list based on HSC marks (cut-offs vary by category/stream).

Reservations as per Govt. norms (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/SBC etc.). Required documents: Marksheets, Leaving Certificate, Caste Certificate (if applicable), Photographs, etc.

6. Facilities & Co-Curricular Activities

  • Well-equipped labs, spacious classrooms, digital library
  • NSS, NCC, Women Development, Self-Defense programs

Students must follow college rules (detailed in Rules & Regulations page). Ragging is strictly prohibited. Anti-ragging committee active. Uniform compulsory. Attendance minimum 75% required as per University rules.
