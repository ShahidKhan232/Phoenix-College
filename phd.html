Doctor of Philosophy (PhD)

Our college offers PhD programs in select disciplines under the affiliation of University of Mumbai. These research-oriented programs focus on original contributions to knowledge in various fields.

PhD Programs Offered

  • PhD in Commerce
  • PhD in Economics
  • PhD in Education
  • PhD in Sociology
  • PhD in Environmental Science (interdisciplinary)

Eligibility

  • Master’s degree (MA/MCom/MSc/MEd etc.) with minimum 55% marks (50% for reserved categories) from a recognized university.
  • Qualified in UGC-NET / SET / JRF / PET (PhD Entrance Test) conducted by University of Mumbai or equivalent.
  • Or M.Phil. degree holders (as per UGC norms).

Duration & Structure

Minimum 3 years (full-time), maximum 6 years. Includes coursework (research methodology, subject-specific papers), synopsis submission, progress reports, pre-PhD presentation, and thesis submission + viva-voce.

Fees (Approximate – 2026-27)

  • Annual Tuition & Registration: ₹25,000 – ₹45,000 (University + College fees)
  • Coursework / Lab fees (if applicable): ₹10,000 – ₹20,000
  • Thesis submission & viva: ₹15,000 – ₹25,000
  • Scholarships/Fellowships: UGC-JRF, University fellowships, or college merit aid available for eligible candidates.

Admission Process

Through University of Mumbai PhD entrance test (PET) or exemption via NET/SET/JRF. Apply via Mumbai University portal when notification is released (usually twice a year). Interviews and research proposal presentation required.

For detailed guidelines, current vacancies, and guides (supervisors), visit the Mumbai University website or contact the Research Cell / Principal's office.
